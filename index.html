Question:

To which famous masterpiece does this detail belong?

Answer:

The moon can be seen in the oil painting "The Starry Night" by Vincent van Gogh. Van Gogh painted the picture in June 1889 and it can be seen today in the Museum of Modern Art in New York.

  • 19th century
  • Painting
  • France

Question:

To which famous masterpiece does this detail belong?

Answer:

Francisco Goya painted "The Third of May" in the year 1814. It's also known as "El tres mayo de 1808 en Madrid and is located in the Museo del Prado in Madrid.

  • 19th century
  • Painting
  • Spain

Question:

To which famous masterpiece does this detail belong?

Answer:

The "Wanderer above the Sea of Fog" was painted by Caspar David Friedrich. It' estimated that it was created around 1808. Today it is on display in the Kunsthalle Hamburg.

  • Painting
  • Germany

To which famous masterpiece does this detail belong?

Answer:
